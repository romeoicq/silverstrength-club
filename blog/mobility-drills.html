5 Best Mobility Drills for Seniors

Simple daily exercises to improve balance, flexibility, and confidence — no equipment needed.

By SilverStrength Club

Published: May 14, 2026

Mobility is the foundation of independence. These 5 drills require no equipment and can be done from your living room. Spend 10-15 minutes daily to see real improvements in balance, flexibility, and confidence.

  1. Ankle Circles — While seated, lift one foot and rotate your ankle 10 times each direction. Improves ankle flexibility and prevents tripping.
  2. Seated Knee Lifts — Sit upright, lift one knee toward your chest, lower, repeat with the other. 10 per side. Builds hip mobility.
  3. Standing March (with support) — Hold a sturdy chair. March in place slowly. 10-20 steps. Great for balance.
  4. Heel-to-Toe Walk — Walk 10 steps with heel touching toes. Use a counter for balance. Classic fall prevention drill.
  5. Side Leg Raises — Hold a chair. Lift one leg sideways, lower slowly. 8-10 per side. Strengthens hips.
